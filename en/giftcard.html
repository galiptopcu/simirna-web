🎁 Gift Card Program

Gift Card

A Branded Physical & Digital Gift Card Program

An end-to-end platform that lets companies create, sell and manage the use of their own branded gift cards. Issue them as physical cards or digital codes, and let customers easily redeem them in your stores via register integration, POS, or mobile app. Boost customer loyalty, grow revenue, and put your brand front and center with every gift.

Get in Touch → Back to Home
🎨
Branded Card Design
Custom design, printing and issuance for your company. Create different card designs themed for seasons, campaigns or special occasions.
💳
Physical & Digital Card
A chip or barcode physical card for in-store sale, or a digital code delivered instantly via email/SMS. Manage both at the same time.
🖥️
Checkout Integration
Instant balance inquiry and redemption via barcode scanner or register software — letting cashiers accept gift cards with zero extra effort.
📱
POS & Mobile Payment
Contactless or chip use at the POS device through simiPOS integration, or payment from the customer's own phone via QR code.
🔄
Balance & Validity Management
Support for expiration dates, partial spending and remaining balance inquiries. Make balance tracking easier for customers via SMS or mobile app.
📊
Central Management & Reporting
Tracking of card sales, usage rates and unused balance (breakage). Comprehensive reports for campaign analytics and revenue optimization.
Use Cases
🛍️
Retail Chain
  • Physical gift card sales on store shelves
  • Birthday, New Year and holiday campaigns
  • Shared use across all branches and online channels
  • Option to issue a gift card instead of a refund
🌐
E-Commerce & Digital
  • Instant digital code sales from the website
  • Instant delivery via email or SMS
  • Card code entry on the online payment page
  • QR code usage via mobile app
🏢
Corporate & B2B
  • Bulk purchases for employee rewards and New Year gifts
  • Customer incentive and promotional campaigns
  • Bulk code issuance with a single invoice
  • Usage tracking via a corporate dashboard
🎯 Who Is It For?
  • Retail chains that want to launch their own branded gift card program
  • E-commerce sites and omnichannel stores
  • Restaurant, café and food chains
  • Institutions that want to give bulk gift cards to their employees
  • Brands that want to use gift codes in customer campaigns
✨ Platform Benefits
  • Revenue assurance through store-bound spending instead of cash
  • Revenue from unused balances (breakage)
  • New customer acquisition — gift recipients visit the store for the first time
  • Increasing average basket size (spending beyond the card value)
  • Brand awareness — advertising value as the card is carried in customers' wallets
  • Single-platform management across physical, digital and POS channels
