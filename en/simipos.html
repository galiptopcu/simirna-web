Industry Challenges
⚠️
Applications need to be rewritten from scratch for every POS/ÖKC brand and model.
⚠️
POS/ÖKC development can't be debugged in a desktop environment, which extends development time.
⚠️
Since unit tests can't be written for POS/ÖKC applications, testing is entirely manual and depends on individuals.
⚠️
Application developers have to learn the SDK of every POS/ÖKC brand and model separately.
