Campus Card

Closed-Loop Payment Solution for Universities and Campuses

Campus Card is a closed-loop payment solution designed specifically for universities and campuses. Manage all on-campus payments — through student and staff cards — on a single platform. Make every campus service, from dining halls to libraries, sports facilities to photocopy machines, accessible with a single card.

👤
Student & Staff Card Management
Separate authorization levels and balance pools for students, academic staff and administrative personnel.
🍽️
Dining Hall & Canteen Integration
Fast access, daily limit definitions and meal-based restriction support.
📚
Library Integration
Book borrowing, late fees and resource reservations on a single platform.
📱
Mobile App Support
A mobile app with balance top-up, transaction history and QR payment features.

Central Management Panel
Monitor, report on and manage every campus location from a single panel.

QR & Contactless Payment
