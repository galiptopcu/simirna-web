💳 Closed-Loop Payment Solution

simiPayment

From Card Production to Payment Solutions — End-to-End Closed-Loop Payment

simiPayment is a closed-loop payment solution. It lets you create your own branded card, manage your merchants, and accept payments via chip, contactless or QR code — bringing your entire payment infrastructure together on a single platform.
