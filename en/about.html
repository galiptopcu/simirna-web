Our Story

Two Decades of Payment Technology

Simirna Technology is a technology company specializing in payment technologies. From our R&D center at Fırat Technopark in Elazığ, we develop deeply specialized solutions in closed-loop payment systems, POS application development and card lifecycle management.

Today we serve a broad customer base across many sectors in Turkey, from fintech to retail chains, university campuses to meal card companies.
