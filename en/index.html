FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which sectors does simiPayment serve?
simiPayment can be adapted to any sector that needs a closed-loop payment system — including retail, food & beverage, healthcare, education, automotive and banking. Thanks to its modular structure, sector-specific customization can be done quickly.
What is a closed-loop payment system?
A closed-loop payment system is a payment infrastructure that is valid within a specific network and can only be used at contracted locations. Campus cards, meal cards and loyalty cards are examples. Compared to open card systems, it offers lower cost and full control.
How long does the integration process take?
A standard simiPOS integration is completed in 2–4 weeks, while a full closed-loop payment system setup takes 8–16 weeks. A detailed project plan is provided after the needs analysis.
Do you provide technical support?
Yes. We provide 24/7 technical support for all our products. We ensure your systems run without interruption through SLA-backed response times and proactive system monitoring.
